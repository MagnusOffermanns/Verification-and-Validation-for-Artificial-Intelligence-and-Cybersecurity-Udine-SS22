>[!Note] Promise
>A formula $\psi \in \phi_\varphi$ is said to [[Promise]] a formula $r \iff \psi$ has one of the following forms:
>- $F(r)$
>- $(p)U(r)$
>- $\neg G \neg r$

 An [[Atom]] $A$ fullfills a formula $\psi$, that [Promises](Promise.md) $r$, if $\neg \psi \in A$ or $r \in A$
